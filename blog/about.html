About Me

Just a little bit about Myself. I'm 25, I've lived in Florida since I graduated from Wells College (if you have heard of it let me know, it would be a surprise) with a degree in philosophy. I had originally moved here to work as Mortgage Loan Officer. After about a year and a half of that, I moved to business Banking with Bank of America. A few months after I started at the bank, I started rock climbing as a hobby, so while at DBC in New York there is a climbing gym in Brooklyn I'd like to check out once if I have time. I started learning code about September of 2014. I started to get a bit more serious about learning code in January 2015, and that pretty much brings me to today. If you want to find out me personally, you can see me on Facebook.

For the pre-college stuff, I'm originally from Niagara Falls, New York. People from my hometown have told me I talk more like a Canadian though (although I don't say 'eh?'). I developed a fear of heights somewhere along the way, which I think makes rock climbing the perfect hobby for me (sometimes). Other hobbies I have had include playing music. I know how to play cello, although I haven't been doing that much while in Florida. I really enjoy playing music but haven't had much time for it recently. I can also play electric Bass, Guitar, and a very little bit of Piano. I picked up swimming late in high school, and swam throughout college as well.

My goals for DBC are to ultimately find a job as a dev in either Seattle, Boston, DC, or Denver. I've never been to Seattle but I think it looks like a terrific place. I did visit the Denver area earlier this year (after being accepted into DBC) to see if I would like living there. I thought the area was amazing. A friend of mine who completed the DBC program works as a dev in Boston, so it would be nice to move somewhere where I already know people and that is part of the appeal of that area. For DC, I have cousins that live a little outside of the DC area, so I wouldn't mind living there either.
