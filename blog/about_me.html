About Me

Just a little bit about Myself. I'm 25, I've lived in Florida since I graduated from Wells College with a degree in philosophy. I have originally moved her to work as Mortgage Loan Officer. After about a year and a half of that, I moved to business Banking with Bank of America. A few months after I started at the bank, I started rock climbing as a hobby, so while at DBC in New York there is a climbing gym in brooklyn I'd like to check out once if I have time. I started learning code about September of 2014. I started to get a bit more serious about learning code in January 2015, and that pretty much brings me to today. If you want to find out me personally, you can see me on Facebook.

My goals for DBC are to ultimately find a job as a dev in either Seattle, Boston, DC, or Denver. I've never been to Seattle but I think it looks like a terrific place. I did visit the Denver area earlier this year (after being accepted into DBC) to see if I would like living there. I thought the area was amazing. A friend of mine who completed the DBC program works as a dev in Boston, so it would be nice to move somewhere where I already know people and that is part of the appeal of that area. For DC, I have cousins that live a little outside of the DC area, so I wouldn't mind living there either.

To see the coding I've done while in DBC, I invite you to check out my projects on GitHub. I haven't pushed any of the things I worked on prior to the program becuase I want to keep them seperate for the time being.
